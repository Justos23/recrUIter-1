Welcome to the RecrUIter

Searching for a job might be stressfull sometimes. You might not be sure what you want to do, and with the pressure of society you do not want to make a decision that will please the society, but the dicision that will lead you to a happy life. That is why the RecrUIter is there for you. The RecrUIter is a web App that tests your personality traits, values and skills and matches you to your ideal career. Within 10 minutes, you are about to discover more about yourself and your strengths, and you will be ready to take on a fabulous career.


This is Jim. Jim is 23 years old, a recent college graduated with little experience and he is not sure what he wants to do with his life. He has earned a degree in Computer Science and thinks about a career in Software Engineering which does not seem to thrill him much. Jim took the test and was a little surprise to see that the career matching best his personnality and values are in sales. Jim has been enjoing his career in sales for the past 2 years now.





Who are we ?


We are a group of people that want to make people's lives easier. Just like you we know how challenging it is to get into the active life and that is why we created this app. We want you to take advantage of the career that suits you the best so that you can live a happy life.


How does it work ?

This app is easy. You will first need to sign in or register in order to get track of your scores, but you can always take the test without creating an account. Then take the assessment by answering a few questions about yourself, your values, your personnality. Finally, get your results and get matched to great professional careers!
